Welcome to Guayaquil

Explore the Pearl of the Pacific far beyond the typical tourist tracks. Dive into hidden natural reserves, historical sanctuaries, and vibrant local flavors.

Why Hidden Gems?

Guayaquil is a city known for its energy, commercial heartbeat, and iconic spots like Malecón 2000 or Las Peñas. However, its true essence also thrives in quiet, overlooked places. This guide is built to reveal those secret spots that offer exceptional architectural, natural, or cultural value.
